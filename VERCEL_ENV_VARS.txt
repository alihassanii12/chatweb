VERCEL ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES
============================

Add these environment variables to your Vercel project:

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Select your "chatweb" project
3. Go to Settings → Environment Variables
4. Add these variables:

Variable Name: NEXT_PUBLIC_API_URL
Value: https://chatweb-backend-mbmu.onrender.com

Variable Name: NEXT_PUBLIC_WS_URL
Value: wss://chatweb-backend-mbmu.onrender.com

5. Click "Save"
6. Redeploy your frontend (go to Deployments → click the three dots on latest deployment → Redeploy)

After redeployment, your frontend at https://chatweb-eight-delta.vercel.app/ will be connected to your backend!
